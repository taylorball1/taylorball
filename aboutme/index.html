Hobbies and Interets

Before moving to Oxford for Univerisity, I lived in a small town along the east coast in Norfolk where I enjoyed spending time exploring the different beaches close by. In my spare time, I enjoy dog training both in agility and obedience; I frequently attend a local club where I train my three dogs: Simba, Jana (who are both border collies) and Parker, who is a deaf boxer which we rescued last year. During summer I usually try to to travel to new places or spend as much time as possible attempting to paddle board and surf in Devon and Cornwall.
